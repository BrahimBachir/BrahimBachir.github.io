BIO

I will be brief, I do not want to waste your time:

I was a refugee who, like all of us, was born in the wrong place. So I ran away.
I was a biologist who did not fit in the competitive world of science becouse I prefer collaboration to competition.
I was a journalist who wanted to tell stories by analyzing the data and discovering it.
So, for all that, one day I fell in love with the JavaScript exosystem.
